# Updates

Progress reports on Raven's development, published a few times a year. Sponsors receive each update first, along with a private note — see [Support Raven](/docs/support-raven/).

- **[July 2026 — A Compiler, GPUs, and the Road to Beta](/docs/updates/2026-07-raven-on-gpus/)**
  Raven runs on GPUs: the tinygrad port is complete, GPT-2 trains on an H100 at tinygrad's pace, Rune and Kaun were rewritten on typed parameter trees, and three new libraries landed (Munin, Vega, Norn). The beta1 scope is complete.

- **[November 2025 — Alpha Complete, Toward the JIT](/docs/updates/2025-11-alpha-complete/)**
  Alpha1 shipped with three new libraries (Fehu, Saga, Talon), FFT and a full linear algebra suite in Nx, workshops at FunOCaml, two Outreachy internships kicked off — and the decision that set up everything since: porting tinygrad to OCaml.
